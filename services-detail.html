Mental Counseling Method

Psychoanalytic Therapy

Psychoanalytic Therapy explores the unconscious, addressing root causes for lasting change. Uncover insights and foster holistic well-being.

Mindfulness Based Therapy

Mindfulness-Based Therapy (MBT) integrates meditation and cognitive-behavioral techniques, fostering awareness and reducing stress. It empowers individuals for resilient, balanced responses to life's challenges.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a goal-oriented approach, identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors. It's evidence-based, structured, and time-limited, effective for various mental health conditions. Therapists collaborate with clients to challenge distorted thinking, fostering healthier beliefs and coping strategies.

Humanistic Therapy

Humanistic Therapy, rooted in human potential and self-actualization, focuses on the present and personal responsibility. It emphasizes empathy, authenticity, and unconditional positive regard. Client-centered, it values the individual's subjective experience, promoting self-exploration and personal growth. Techniques include active listening, reflection, and open-ended questioning.
